Hypergiant Entertainment Pty Ltd

Hypergiant Entertainment Pty Ltd is a indie video game company based in Wollongong Australia. It was started by Eden Harris who had been meddling with space simulations for a while. He began game development in 2015 and formally created Hypergiant Entertainment Pty Ltd in 2016.

Currently we are making a space simulation engine and game designed to work on PC, Mac, Linux, Mobile and in the Browser.
