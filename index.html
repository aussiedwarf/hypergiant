Hypergiant Entertainment Pty Ltd

Hypergiant Entertainment Pty Ltd is a indie video game company based in Wollongong Australia. It was started by Eden Harris who had been meddling with space simulations for a while. He began game development in 2015 and formally created Hypergiant Entertainment Pty Ltd in 2016. Here we are making nbody.space, a space simulation engine and game.
